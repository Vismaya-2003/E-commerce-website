Who Are We?


We are Glossier, a beauty and skincare brand that celebrates individuality and authenticity. Founded by Emily Weiss, we believe in the power of self-expression and enhancing your unique beauty. Our team of beauty enthusiasts and experts is dedicated to creating products that make you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin. With a focus on simplicity and effectiveness, we provide a range of makeup and skincare essentials that fit seamlessly into your daily routine. At Glossier, we're all about real beauty, real people, and real results.
